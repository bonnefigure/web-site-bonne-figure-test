Bring the Outdoors in

D’un monde devenu impossible, le futur nous permet d’imaginer encore des mondes possibles. Le temps est venu d’explorer, de rêver et de se projeter ce qui serait probable demain. Les rayons du soleil frapperont de plus en plus violemment. L’ombre deviendra notre alliée. Mais qu’adviendra-t-il de notre relation au ciel et au soleil ? From a world that has become impossible, the future still allows us to imagine possible worlds. The time has come to explore, to dream, and to project what might be probable tomorrow. The sun’s rays will strike with increasing intensity. Shade will become our ally. But what will become of our relationship with the sky and the sun?
